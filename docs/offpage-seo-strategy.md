### 2.2 NAP Consistency Guidelines

#### NAP Format Standard

```
Business Name: Flood Doctor
Address: [Street Address], [City], VA [ZIP]
Phone: (XXX) XXX-XXXX
Website: flooddoctor.com
```

#### Critical Rules

1. **Exact Match**: Use identical business name everywhere
   - CORRECT: "Flood Doctor"
   - INCORRECT: "Flood Doctor LLC", "The Flood Doctor", "Flood Doctor Inc."

2. **Address Format**: Choose one format and stick with it
   - CORRECT: "123 Main Street, Suite 100"
   - INCORRECT: "123 Main St., Ste. 100" (on some listings)

3. **Phone Number Format**: Use consistent formatting
   - CORRECT: "(703) 555-1234" everywhere
   - INCORRECT: "703-555-1234" on some, "(703) 555-1234" on others

4. **Tracking Numbers**: Avoid using multiple tracking numbers across directories

#### NAP Audit Checklist

- [ ] Run audit using Moz Local, BrightLocal, or Whitespark
- [ ] Create master spreadsheet of all citations
- [ ] Identify inconsistencies
- [ ] Update incorrect listings (allow 2-4 weeks for propagation)
- [ ] Set quarterly audit reminders
- [ ] Monitor for unauthorized changes

## 3. Content Syndication

### 3.1 Platforms for Republishing Content

#### Primary Syndication Platforms

| Platform | Audience | Best Content Types | SEO Consideration |
|----------|----------|-------------------|-------------------|
| Medium | General/Professional | Long-form articles, guides | Uses canonical tags |
| LinkedIn Articles | B2B/Professional | Industry insights, case studies | No canonical tags |
| Quora Spaces | Q&A seekers | How-to content, expertise | Backlinks in bio |
| Reddit (relevant subs) | Niche communities | Valuable insights, AMA | No self-promotion |

#### Medium Strategy

**Setup:**
1. Create branded publication on Medium
2. Import existing blog posts using Medium's import tool
3. Add relevant tags (water damage, home improvement, insurance)

**Content to Republish:**
- Complete guides (e.g., "Complete Guide to Water Damage Restoration")
- Seasonal content (storm preparation, winter pipe protection)
- Insurance claim guides
- Prevention tips

**Best Practices:**
- Wait 2-7 days after original publication before syndicating
- Use Medium's import tool to preserve canonical tags
- Add custom intro for Medium audience
- Include CTA at end directing to website

#### LinkedIn Articles Strategy

**Setup:**
1. Switch personal profile to Creator Mode
2. Create company page for branded content
3. Build network of insurance agents, property managers, realtors

**Content to Republish:**
- Business-focused content
- Partnership opportunity posts
- Industry trend analysis
- Case studies (with permission)

**Best Practices:**
- Do NOT publish immediately after blog post
- Wait at least 1-2 weeks
- Significantly modify opening paragraph
- LinkedIn does not use canonical tags (risk of outranking original)
- Focus on building thought leadership vs. SEO

### 5.2 FTC Compliance Guidelines (2024 Rule)

#### Prohibited Practices

- Writing fake reviews or paying for fake reviews
- Incentivizing star ratings without disclosure
- Suppressing negative reviews
- Undisclosed employee or family reviews
- Review gating (only asking happy customers)

#### Compliant Practices

- Ask ALL customers for reviews
- Request "honest" feedback
- Never offer discounts for reviews
- Respond professionally to all reviews
- Disclose any relationships

### 6.4 Plumbers and Contractors

#### Referral Network Strategy

**Plumbers**
- First responders to water issues
- Natural referral source
- Reciprocal arrangement possible

**General Contractors**
- Renovation projects uncover water damage
- Reconstruction after restoration
- Long-term partnership potential

**HVAC Companies**
- Water damage from AC units
- Humidity control expertise
- Complementary services

**Roofers**
- Storm damage = water intrusion
- Seasonal partnership (spring/fall)
- Joint marketing during storm season

#### Reciprocal Referral Program

| You Refer | They Refer |
|-----------|------------|
| Plumbing repairs (after restoration) | Water damage calls |
| Reconstruction projects | Flood/water emergencies |
| HVAC maintenance | AC leak situations |
| Roofing inspections | Storm damage cleanup |

#### Partnership Outreach

1. **Identify Local Partners**
   - Check Google Maps for highly-rated local companies
   - Ask current customers who they use
   - Attend local trade association meetings

2. **Initial Meeting**
   - Coffee or lunch meeting
   - Bring business cards and materials
   - Discuss mutual benefit
   - Set clear referral expectations

3. **Maintain Relationship**
   - Quarterly check-ins
   - Holiday appreciation (gift baskets)
   - Joint training opportunities
   - Reciprocal social media promotion
