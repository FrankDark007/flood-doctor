### Data Sources
- Google Keyword Planner (official, but conservative)
- SEMrush Keyword Magic Tool
- Ahrefs Keywords Explorer
- Google Search Autocomplete
- Google SERP Analysis

### Assumptions in This Database
- Volume estimates are monthly search frequency
- Competition ratings are based on SERP difficulty
- CPC estimates are average US rates (may vary by location)
- All keywords ranked by conversion potential for service area

### How to Use This Database

1. **For Content Planning**: Group keywords by target URL to determine what topics to cover
2. **For Priority**: Start with Tier 1 and 2 keywords for fastest SEO ROI
3. **For On-Page**: Include primary keyword in title, H1, and early content
4. **For Internal Linking**: Use secondary keywords as anchor text
5. **For PPC**: Use high-volume, high-intent keywords for paid ads
6. **For Monthly Reports**: Track ranking and traffic for Tier 1 keywords
