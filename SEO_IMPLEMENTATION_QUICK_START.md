## QUESTIONS & TROUBLESHOOTING

**"How long until we see results?"**
- SEO typically shows results 60-90 days from consistent implementation
- Quick wins (local pack) can show in 4-6 weeks
- Organic ranking growth: 3-6 months for competitive keywords

**"What if we don't have time to publish 2 articles/week?"**
- Start with 1 article/week (still effective)
- Ensure quality over quantity
- Gradually increase publishing frequency
- Outsource writing if necessary

**"Should we do paid ads while building organic?"**
- For emergency services: Yes, use local service ads immediately
- For general brand building: Focus organic first
- Once organic stabilizes: Reduce ad spend gradually

**"How do we handle negative reviews/bad publicity?"**
- Respond professionally to all reviews
- Focus on positive content (good reviews, testimonials)
- Build enough quality content that negative content ranks lower
- Never attack or be defensive
