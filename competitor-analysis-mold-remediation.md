## Content Gaps & Opportunities

### Topics Under-Covered by Competitors:
1. **Mold Testing Explained** - Only Valor Mold emphasizes instant testing technology
2. **Cost Transparency** - Most avoid specific pricing; opportunity to be transparent
3. **Insurance Claims Process** - Only SERVPRO/PuroClean mention
4. **Before/After Case Studies** - Only Valor Mold shows visual proof
5. **Video Content** - Only Valor Mold (Fox 5 News feature)
6. **Comparison Content** - "Mold Remediation vs Mold Removal" rarely explained
7. **Seasonal Mold Risks** - None address Virginia climate/humidity patterns
8. **Rental/Landlord Guidance** - None address tenant/landlord responsibilities

### Missing Local Content:
1. **Specific NoVA Neighborhoods** - Limited hyperlocal targeting
2. **Local Humidity/Climate Data** - Virginia-specific mold conditions
3. **Local Building Codes** - Virginia mold remediation regulations
4. **Local Health Department Resources** - Links to Fairfax County, etc.
