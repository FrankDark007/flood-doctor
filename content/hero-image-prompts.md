# Service Page Hero Image Prompts

## Master Template Style

All hero images should follow this consistent style:

```
UNIFORM (professional restoration tech):
- Royal blue polo shirt with small white logo on left chest
- Dark navy cargo pants (clean, professional)
- Clean tan work boots
- Company baseball cap - blue with white logo
- Moisture meter or relevant tool clipped to belt
- NO high-vis vest

COMPOSITION (for "head above card" effect):
- Worker in lower 2/3 of frame, sky fills top 1/3
- Camera at waist height, slight upward angle
- Worker offset to left or right side of frame
- Walking/working at 3/4 angle, sense of motion

LIGHTING:
- Golden hour, sun behind/beside worker creating rim light
- Face in soft shadow, looking off to side - NOT at camera
- Warm gradient sky

TECHNICAL:
- 4:3 ratio, 1600x1200px
- 85mm, f/2.0
- Documentary style like trade magazine editorial
- NO text, logos, or watermarks visible
```
