## Competitive Gap Analysis: What We Need to Match or Beat

### MUST HAVE (All competitors have these):
1. **24/7 Emergency messaging** - prominent, repeated throughout page
2. **Phone number** - highly visible, clickable, in header
3. **IICRC Certification** - mentioned with logo
4. **Service area coverage** - specific cities/neighborhoods listed
5. **Water extraction/drying/dehumidification** - core services explained
6. **Mold remediation tie-in** - as related service
7. **Insurance assistance messaging** - claims help mentioned
8. **Schema markup** - LocalBusiness at minimum
9. **Mobile-responsive design** - all competitors have this
10. **Process explanation** - steps or methodology shown

### SHOULD HAVE (Most competitors have these):
1. **Multiple H2 sections** - 5-7 minimum for SEO depth
2. **Customer testimonials** - with names and locations
3. **Response time promise** - "within X minutes/hours"
4. **Google review rating display** - stars + count
5. **Physical address** - builds local trust
6. **Commercial + Residential** - both audiences addressed
7. **ZIP/location finder** - franchise model standard
8. **Proprietary system/process name** - QuickDry, etc.
9. **Owner/team info** - for local franchises
10. **Word count 1,500+** - content depth for SEO

### DIFFERENTIATION OPPORTUNITIES (Gaps to exploit):
1. **FAQ Section** - Only RestorTech has one; major opportunity
2. **Video content** - Minimal across competitors
3. **Cost/pricing transparency** - None address this
4. **Response time guarantee** - Few make specific promises
5. **Before/after galleries** - Not prominent on main pages
6. **Live chat** - Only PuroClean has AI chatbot
7. **Detailed case studies** - Missing from most
8. **Educational blog integration** - Weak across board
9. **Speed/performance metrics** - Vague claims only
10. **Guarantee specifics** - Most are implied, not explicit
