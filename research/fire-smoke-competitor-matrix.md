### Paul Davis Northern Virginia
- **Strengths:** Strong insurance relationship messaging, 30-minute response guarantee, eco-friendly methods highlighted
- **Unique Elements:** Direct insurance billing emphasis, reconstruction capabilities

## Competitive Gap Opportunities

### Content Gaps No Competitor Addresses Well

1. **Cost transparency** - No competitor provides pricing guidance
2. **Timeline expectations** - Vague about project duration
3. **Health risks** - Minimal coverage of smoke inhalation dangers
4. **Insurance claim tips** - Generic insurance language, no specific guidance
5. **DIY risks** - Why professional help is necessary
6. **Commercial-specific content** - Most focus heavily on residential
7. **Before/after galleries** - Visual proof of work lacking
8. **Video content** - No competitors have embedded video

1. **FAQ Schema** - Only PuroClean has comprehensive FAQs
