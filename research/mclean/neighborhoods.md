## Housing Stock Analysis

### Estate Homes (Primary Target)
- **Size Range:** 4,000 - 15,000+ sq ft
- **Lot Size:** 0.5 - 5+ acres
- **Age:** Mix of 1950s-2000s builds
- **Common Features:**
  - Finished basements with:
    - Wine cellars
    - Home theaters
    - Home offices
    - Recreation rooms
  - Custom millwork
  - Hardwood floors
  - Art collections
  - Pool houses/cabanas
  - Separate guest quarters

### Water Damage Vulnerabilities

| Feature | Risk Factor | Service Opportunity |
|---------|-------------|---------------------|
| Finished basements | Sump pump failure, foundation seepage | Emergency pumping, structural drying |
| Wine cellars | Humidity, temperature fluctuation | Specialized drying, climate restoration |
| Home theaters | Electronics damage, acoustic materials | Quick extraction, electronics protection |
| Pool houses | Pipe bursts, freeze damage | Winterization, emergency response |
| Private wells | Pump failure, contamination | Water testing, system coordination |
| Private septic | Backup, overflow | Category 3 cleanup |
| Mature trees | Root intrusion, sewer damage | Coordinated plumbing repair |
