## McLean-Specific Water Damage Risks

### Based on Local Housing Stock & Geography

#### 1. Finished Basement Flooding (PRIMARY RISK)
McLean's 4,000-15,000 sq ft estate homes feature extensive below-grade amenities:
- Wine cellars with humidity control systems
- Home theaters with acoustic materials and electronics
- Home offices with IT equipment
- Recreation rooms with custom finishes
- Guest quarters and in-law suites

**Search Intent Keywords:**
- "basement flooding McLean VA"
- "finished basement water damage"
- "sump pump failure McLean"
- "basement waterproofing McLean Virginia"

#### 2. Hidden Leak Damage
Slow leaks in large homes create hidden moisture that:
- Weakens drywall
- Swells wood framing
- Reduces insulation effectiveness
- Fosters mold growth within 24-48 hours
- Often undetected until structural separation or discoloration appears

**Search Intent Keywords:**
- "hidden water leak McLean"
- "mold from water leak"
- "how to find water leak in walls"

#### 3. Tree Root Intrusions
Mature oaks and maples in older neighborhoods (Chesterbrook, Langley):
- Invade sewer lines through tiny fissures
- Cause clogs, backups, sewage overflows
- Especially problematic in clay soils

**Search Intent Keywords:**
- "tree root sewer damage McLean"
- "sewage backup cleanup McLean VA"
- "sewer line repair Northern Virginia"

#### 4. Clay Soil Challenges
Northern Virginia's clay-heavy soils:
- Expand when wet, contract when dry
- Shift foundations over time
- Create cracks for water entry
- Worsen with seasonal rain patterns

**Search Intent Keywords:**
- "foundation leak McLean"
- "water in basement McLean VA"
- "crawl space water damage"

#### 5. High Water Table Near Potomac
Low-lying areas and Potomac proximity:
- Persistent humidity issues
- Basement flooding during heavy rains
- Septic system challenges
- Yard saturation affecting foundations

**Search Intent Keywords:**
- "Potomac area flooding"
- "water table basement flooding"
- "flood zone water damage McLean"

#### 6. Frozen Pipe Bursts (Winter)
Winter risks in older large homes:
- Uninsulated pipe runs in attics/crawlspaces
- Pool house and cabana freeze damage
- Guest house/outbuilding vulnerabilities

**Search Intent Keywords:**
- "frozen pipes McLean VA"
- "burst pipe repair Northern Virginia"
- "emergency plumber McLean"

#### 7. Pool House & Outdoor Structures
Estate properties with pool houses, cabanas:
- Seasonal pipe bursts
- Irrigation system failures
- Outdoor kitchen plumbing issues

**Search Intent Keywords:**
- "pool house water damage"
