## WHY AI MODELS RECOMMEND THESE SPECIFIC COMPANIES

### Primary Reasons (in order of importance):

1. **They exist in AI training data** - These companies have been online longer with consistent content
2. **Clear geographic signals** - City names appear prominently in structured ways
3. **Crawlable websites** - Content is accessible to web crawlers that feed AI models
4. **Consistent NAP** - Name, Address, Phone appear identically across the web
5. **Citation network** - They're mentioned on multiple authoritative sites
6. **Structured data** - Schema markup helps AI extract key facts
7. **Fresh content** - Regular updates signal active business
8. **Reviews** - Aggregated ratings from Google, Yelp, etc.

### What Flood Doctor Lacks:

1. **Crawlable presence** - React SPA may not be fully indexed
2. **Citation network** - Limited directory presence
3. **AI training data** - Company may not appear in training corpora
4. **Structured data gaps** - Schema may be incomplete
5. **Explicit geo-targeting** - SAB model limits address visibility
